== Description ==
Polity Lite is stylish, modern, professional and sophisticated campaign WordPress theme custom-made for politicians and public servants. It is a multi-purpose theme can be used for social movements, political parties, charity, NGOs, fundraisers, crowdfunding campaigns, grassroots and other websites related to politics and social movement. With this high-quality political theme you can easily create a candidate website that will kick off your political campaigns. It can help you to create your online space in a snap of a finger. You can create a strong impact on your political area and reach a much wider audience.
